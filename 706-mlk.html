706 MLK Jr. Drive

A home with history — restored for the next generation of leaders in Southeast Greensboro.

The Story

From a legacy of advocacy to a home for what's next.

Purchased for $100,000, this 5-bedroom, 2-bathroom, 2,000 sq. ft. home underwent a $60,000 renovation, transforming it into a modern, inviting space.

706 MLK carries real history — it was the former home of Neddie Coid, an advocate for racial equity. Today it welcomes a new generation of young professionals, activists, and leaders to Greensboro, continuing the work she stood for.
