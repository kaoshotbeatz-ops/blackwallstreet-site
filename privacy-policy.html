Privacy Policy

Your trust is the foundation of everything we build. Here is exactly how we handle your information.

Last updated June 19, 2026

Black Wall Street LLC ("Black Wall Street," "we," "us," or "our") is a community-focused real estate and development firm based in Greensboro, North Carolina. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website at blackwallstreetllc.org, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to respond to you and operate our site responsibly.

Information you give us

  • Contact details you submit through our forms — name, email address, and phone number
  • The subject of your inquiry (for example, homeownership, an investment or partnership, a development project, or REI workshops)
  • Any message, question, or property information you choose to share with us
  • Newsletter or community-update sign-up information, if you opt in

Information collected automatically

  • Basic, aggregated analytics such as pages visited, approximate region, browser type, and referring source
  • Standard server log data (IP address, date and time of request) used for security and reliability

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries about homeownership, investment, partnership, and development opportunities
  • To guide prospective buyers and partners through the steps that follow an inquiry
  • To send community updates, workshop information, or newsletters when you have asked us to
  • To understand how our site is used so we can improve it
  • To protect the security and integrity of our website

We Do Not Sell Your Information

Black Wall Street LLC does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We are a community-rooted firm, and we treat the information you share with the same care we bring to the neighborhoods we rebuild.

Cookies & Analytics

Our site may use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working properly and to gather anonymous, aggregated usage statistics. These help us see which information is most useful to homeowners, investors, and partners. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent; some parts of the site may not function as smoothly if you do.

Third-Party Services

We rely on a small number of trusted service providers to operate our site — for example, web hosting, embedded maps, and analytics. These providers may process limited data on our behalf and only as needed to deliver their service. Our site may also embed or link to third-party platforms (such as our social media pages). Those platforms have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information you share. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so while we work to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You may ask us what personal information we hold about you
  • You may request that we correct or delete your information
  • You may opt out of community emails or newsletters at any time
  • You may decline cookies through your browser settings

To exercise any of these choices, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for adults exploring homeownership, investment, and community development. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date above. Significant changes will be reflected on this page.
