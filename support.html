What to expect

We treat every inquiry with care. Here's what you can expect after you reach out.

We aim to respond to email and contact-form messages within 1–2 business days. For time-sensitive matters — like an active homeownership step or a development deadline — calling us is the quickest path. When you write, including a few details up front helps us help you faster:

  • — Whether you're a homeowner, prospective buyer, investor, or partner
  • — The property or neighborhood involved, if any
  • — What you're hoping to accomplish or the question you have
  • — The best way and time to reach you
Common Questions

Frequently asked

I'm interested in buying a home. Where do I start?

Reach out by email or phone and tell us a little about what you're looking for. Our team can walk you through available opportunities and the homeownership pathways we support, including first-time buyer guidance. See our lending and financial guidance for more.

I want to invest or partner with Black Wall Street. How?

We work with aligned investors and community partners on residential and commercial development. Email us with a brief note about your interests and we'll set up a conversation. You can also join the movement to stay connected.

How do I register for an REI workshop?

Visit our REI Workshops page for current sessions and details, or contact us and we'll make sure you have the information you need to take part.

Do you only work in Greensboro?

Greensboro, North Carolina is our home base and the heart of our work. If you have an opportunity nearby, reach out — we're always glad to talk through what's possible.

Is the information on this site financial or legal advice?

No. Everything on our site is general and informational. For advice tailored to your situation, please consult a qualified professional. See our legal notices for details.
