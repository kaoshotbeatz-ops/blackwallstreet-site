Why It Matters

Real estate education that builds generational wealth.

For too long, the knowledge that builds wealth has stayed behind closed doors. REI Workshops open those doors — demystifying financing, ownership, and investment so the community can build, own, and pass it on.

Whether you're buying your first home or your first investment property, you'll leave with a plan and the confidence to act on it.

When you teach a community to own, you change its future.

— REI Workshops

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions.

Who are the workshops for?

Anyone — first-time buyers, renters dreaming of ownership, and aspiring investors. No prior experience needed.

How much do they cost?

Pricing and schedules are announced per session. Register your interest below and we'll notify you of the next workshop.

Where are they held?

Workshops are based in Greensboro, NC, with virtual options as they become available.

Do you help after the workshop?

Yes. Our lending specialists and brokers can guide you well beyond the classroom — all the way to the closing table.
